The movie therefore provides a positive-negative (or, on balance, neutral) stimulus from which to evaluate the public discourse about Russia in more than 40 foreign languages and 11 individual languages. The movie's usage of positive and negative stimuli also includes references to individual articles or media. The results suggest that foreign policy discourse in Europe is more negative than the Russian version and that Russia's public discourse is more negative than that of European countries.

Corruption in underdeveloped countries is a huge concern among environmentalists and human rights groups. Their concerns are partly why this round of climate negotiations has been focused on transparency and reducing emissions – two areas where developed countries have few resources.

"Whatever our private disagreements on the adequacy of this agreement, we are certain that the momentum for global action to cut emissions and our shared ambition to bring greater good to our communities and nations alike will not be lost," said a spokesperson.

Incitement to cross-language problems (EuL) refers to an attacker's spread of hateful material through social media and has become a serious problem across Europe. It is particularly relevant for the many Roma people (hepatophiles) who run Google, Facebook, Twitter and other social networks and regularly promote attacks against the Roma community.

Here in Germany, many law enforcement agencies receive information of attacks against the Roma community. They are not established to investigate these reports, though, because the government has not found the necessary resources for a large-scale, multifaceted anti-hate campaign.

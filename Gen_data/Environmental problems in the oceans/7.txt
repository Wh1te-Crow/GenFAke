Environmental problems in the oceans are a crisis," says marine scientist Dr Roberto Marin. "Our countries need to work together to conserve the oceans and stop the accumulation of trash in them."

While all efforts must be made to reduce the quantity of waste in the oceans, marine pollution is a "tragedy of the commons" as the scale of the issue is too big for any single country to solve alone.

By now, all countries in the world, especially industrialised ones, should recognise that pollution in the oceans has consequences for marine life and the food chain, for human health and for the resources that our nations use.

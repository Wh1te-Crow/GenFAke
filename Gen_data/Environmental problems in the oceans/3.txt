Environmental problems in the oceans?

Today, 6.3% of the world's gross water resources are consumed by the oceans. A significant proportion of that is caused by pollution of the ocean's environment.

Human activities, especially in recent decades, have contributed to rapid growth of plastic pollution in the oceans.

Climate change?

Climate change is a major threat to biodiversity and ecosystem services.

If we keep up our present rate of emission of carbon dioxide, temperatures may rise by 4.5 °C or more in this century and temperatures of 10 °C are more likely to be reached in the 21st century.

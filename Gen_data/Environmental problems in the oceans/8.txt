Environmental problems in the oceans are a major part of our lives.

Between 5 and 10 percent of all fish caught worldwide are discarded or simply never even reach the market. Bycatch is associated with many of the worst sorts of fish entanglements. Haida Gwaii and Vancouver Island in Canada are some of the worst hotspots. In 2011, the following percent of all puffer fish caught and returned: 1.5 percent on Haida Gwaii, 2.1 percent on Vancouver Island, 2.9 percent on Prince Edward Island.
